About

AMPRSND is a creative studio by Isaiah Steinberg and Bianca Nicdao.

Their collaboration began inside the music industry and expanded outward across sound, image, and experience.

Based between Toronto and Los Angeles, working in focused projects and campaign-scale ideas.
